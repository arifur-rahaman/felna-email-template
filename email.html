Join Hands with Felna: Your Trusted Recycling Partner

Dear Convenors,

I hope this message finds you well. On behalf of Felna, I am excited to express our interest in becoming the official recycling partner for the upcoming concert organized by Black & White Communication (BW) and Shah Tries To Sing (STTS) on 6th January 2025 at Zilla Shilpakala Academy, Chattogram.

Felna is a waste management company dedicated to promoting sustainability through the principles of Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. We are eager to support this event by implementing sustainable waste management practices and raising environmental awareness among the attendees.

As your recycling partner, Felna will:

  • Set up designated recycling bins throughout the venue to enable proper waste segregation.
  • Deploy trained volunteers to assist attendees in responsibly disposing of waste and educate them on eco-friendly habits.
  • Collect plastic bottles and wrappers directly from visitors for recycling, ensuring a clean and green event.
  • Distribute awareness materials, such as brochures and stickers, to encourage attendees to adopt sustainable practices.

Together, we can make a significant impact by reducing waste and encouraging recycling in our community.
